About Live Crisis Radar

Live Crisis Radar is a global real-time crisis tracking platform designed to visualize major world events as they happen.

Our mission is to make global disaster and crisis monitoring more visual, interactive, and accessible for everyone.

The platform tracks major earthquakes, hurricanes, wildfires, conflicts, and other significant world events using live data feeds and real-time monitoring systems.

Live Crisis Radar was created to help users quickly understand what is happening around the world through a clean, map-based interface.

Features

  • Real-time global earthquake monitoring
  • Live disaster visualization
  • Conflict and crisis tracking
  • Interactive world map
  • Global event filtering system

Data Sources

Data may be sourced from public monitoring services, weather organizations, geological agencies, and humanitarian reporting systems.
